ABOUT

US

Royal Bags Corporation established in the year 1971 by Sayyed Ahmad Ali, it was the first manufacturing unit of promotional and packing bags in the heart of Madhya Pradesh INDORE.

He started with the aim of providing efficient and smart promotional means to the growing business of Indore and to generate employment for women and needy. RBC gained enormous popularity from the renowned brands under his leadership.


Adapting to the dynamic landscape of time, technology, and customer preferences, today we manufacture over 100 thousand bags monthly. We excel in delivering top-notch printing, tested strength, and durability in our bags through production expertise, skilled labor, and advanced machinery.

Experience

with the experience of over 50 years in the craft of manufacturing top-tier promotional bags, our journey reflects a rich legacy of commitment and excellence.
